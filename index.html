Alek Turkmen is currently a junior at The Cooper Union for the Advancement of Science and Art studying Mechanical Engineering with an intended minor in Economics and Public Policy. Alek has three internships under his belt, ranging from NYC's MEP consulting industry, environmental sensor engineering with IoT technology in NYC's East Village, and manufacturing of airborne radar for mission-critical and space operations.

Alek is highly experience in both front/back-end technologies. For CADing and sim, Alek has worked with and has complete mastery of AutoCAD, Revit, Inventor Pro, SOLIDWORKS, and NX/Siemens. For coding and analysis, Alek has strong involvement in HTML/CSS/JS, Python, SQL, Excel/Minitab, GR&R, and MATLAB.

Finally, Alek's interests lie in startups and small tech and he envisions himself owning his own company in the future.
