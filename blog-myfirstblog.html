Introduction to myself

Hi, my name is Alek Turkmen. I’m currently 20 years old and I’m about to go into my junior year of college at The Cooper Union. I’ve always loved math, science and problem solving, but I was never able to pinpoint a precise career interest so I decided to study mechanical engineering as a broad way to encompass my interests. I am very curious by nature and one of the first things someone notices when getting to know me is how straightforward I am.

I’m originally from Istanbul, Turkey (although I was born in the States) so I have a pretty holistic view of the world and get along well with international people, not just natives. My first language was Turkish but since I moved to the US when I was 7, I’ve since forgotten most of it and my English is so good you wouldn’t be able to tell it wasn’t my first language.

I’m introverted by nature, however I’m a better extrovert. I like to think I’ve struck a great balance between the two since I can be the life of a party, but I prefer to be alone. Speaking of being alone, I really think it's important to be able to think in one's consciousness for extended periods of time without getting bored. I get my ideas, direction and (trying not to sound braggy but also) my intelligence from how disciplined my mind is.
